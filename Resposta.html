Início Aulas Exercícios Dicas Jogos

Resposta dos exercícios:

1. Complete as frases com o verbo "to be" no presente simples (am, is, are):
    a) She is a doctor.
    b) They are from Brazil.
    c) I am happy today.
2. Escreva a forma negativa das frases no presente simples:
    a) He does not (doesn't) work in an office.
    b) We do not (don't) live in Paris.
    c) They do not (don't) like football.
3. Coloque o verbo entre parênteses no presente contínuo:
    a) She is studying for her exam.
    b) We are playing soccer in the park.
    c) They are traveling to New York.
4. Conjugue os verbos entre parênteses no passado simples:
    a) I visited my grandparents last weekend.
    b) She went to the gym yesterday.
    c) They saw a movie last night.
5. Complete as frases com "will" ou "going to" para falar de futuro:
    a) I think it will rain tomorrow.
    b) We are going to visit our cousins next weekend.
    c) She won't (will not) come to the meeting.
6. Transforme as frases abaixo em perguntas no passado simples:
    a) Did you finish the homework?
    b) Did they go to the concert?
    c) Did he call his friend?
7. Coloque os advérbios de frequência no lugar correto (always, never, often):
    a) She is always late to work.
    b) We often eat pizza on Fridays.
    c) They always watch TV after dinner.
8. Complete com os pronomes possessivos corretos (my, your, his, her, our, their):
    a) This is my book.
    b) Is that their car?
    c) We are going to visit our parents tomorrow.
9. Complete as frases com o verbo correto no passado contínuo:
    a) I was reading a book when the phone rang.
    b) They were watching TV when we arrived.
    c) She was cooking dinner when the guests came.
10. Escreva 3 frases sobre o que você vai fazer no futuro usando "will" ou "going to":
    a) I will travel to Japan next year.
    b) She is going to study English this afternoon.
    c) We will meet our friends tomorrow.
