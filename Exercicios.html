Início Aulas Exercícios Dicas Jogos

Temos 10 exercícios para a prática do conteúdo que está disponível em aulas

Resposta dos exercícios

1. Complete as frases com o verbo "to be" no presente simples (am, is, are):
    a) She ___ a doctor.
    b) They ___ from Brazil.
    c) I ___ happy today.
2. Escreva a forma negativa das frases no presente simples:
    a) He works in an office.
    b) We live in Paris.
    c) They like football.
3. Coloque o verbo entre parênteses no presente contínuo:
    a) She (study) _________ for her exam.
    b) We (play) _________ soccer in the park.
    c) They (travel) _________ to New York.
4. Conjugue os verbos entre parênteses no passado simples:
    a) I (visit) ________ my grandparents last weekend.
    b) She (go) ________ to the gym yesterday.
    c) They (see) ________ a movie last night.
5. Complete as frases com "will" ou "going to" para falar de futuro:
    a) I think it _________ (rain) tomorrow.
    b) We _________ (visit) our cousins next weekend.
    c) She _________ (not/come) to the meeting.
6. Transforme as frases abaixo em perguntas no passado simples:
    a) You finished the homework.
    b) They went to the concert.
    c) He called his friend.
7. Coloque os advérbios de frequência no lugar correto (always, never, often):
    a) She is late to work.
    b) We eat pizza on Fridays.
    c) They watch TV after dinner.
8. Complete com os pronomes possessivos corretos (my, your, his, her, our, their):
    a) This is _______ book. (meu)
    b) Is that _______ car? (deles)
    c) We are going to visit _______ parents tomorrow. (nossos)
9. Complete as frases com o verbo correto no passado contínuo:
    a) I _______ (read) a book when the phone rang.
    b) They _______ (watch) TV when we arrived.
    c) She _______ (cook) dinner when the guests came.
